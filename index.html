Interstellar is a 2014 film from Christopher Nolan, of which he directed, co-wrote, and co-produced. It stars Mathew McCaonaughey, Michael Caine, Anne Hathaway, David Gyasi, and Wes Bentley. Set in a near distopian future, where a disastrous blight has been slowly destroying crops and rendering earth uninhabitable, humanity must find a way to persist. Throughout the film, we watch the crew of The Endurance make their way across the solar system to a mysterious wormhole in an attempt to find a habitable planet for humanity.

Interstellar is a science fiction film set in a near future dystopian earth where nearly all natural resources have been rendered unable to be cultivated due to dust storms ravaging the world. The film follows Cooper, a former engineer and NASA pilot who was forced to give up what he loved doing to become a farmer. After him and his daughter experience an anomoly of unknown origin, they stumble upon a secret NASA facility led by Professor Brand who reveals their plan to save humanity. Brand and the other scientists working at this facility have discovered a wormhole leading to a whole other galaxy. With the posibility of a habitable planet on the other side, Cooper is recruited as the pilot for this mission, to steer their ship, the Endurance, along side a team of scientists including Brands daughter Amelia, physicist Romilly, biologist Boyle, and two ex military robots, TARS and CASE. While Cooper must leave his family behind, communication becomes distant as they battle with time dialation. As they explore uncharted territory of humanknowledge, they must make decisions that determine the fate of humanity. Interstellar is a captivating tale of exploration, sacrafice and human determination, all while having phenominal and scientifically accurate graphics.
